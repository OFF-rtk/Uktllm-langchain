## üé° Tourist Attractions & Timings

### 1. Bagnath Temple (The Core)
* **Type:** Religious / Historical
* **Location:** At the confluence of Saryu and Gomti rivers (Sangam).
* **Timings:** 5:00 AM ‚Äì 9:00 PM.
* **Significance:** Dedicated to Lord Shiva in his "Tiger" (Bagh) avatar. Built in 1450 AD by Laxmi Chand.
* **Key Notes:** This is the heart of the town. The evening *Aarti* at the Saryu Ghat is peaceful but simpler than Haridwar's.

### 2. Chandika Temple
* **Type:** Religious / Viewpoint
* **Location:** 2 km uphill from the main market.
* **Timings:** Sunrise to Sunset.
* **Significance:** Dedicated to Goddess Chandika.
* **Key Notes:** Offers a panoramic bird's-eye view of the entire Bageshwar valley and the river confluence.

### 3. Baijnath Temple Complex
* **Type:** Archaeological / Heritage
* **Location:** 21 km from Bageshwar (towards Kausani).
* **Timings:** 6:00 AM ‚Äì 7:00 PM.
* **Significance:** Ancient capital of the Katyuri Kings. Famous for stone idols and the Golden Mahseer fish in the river.
* **Key Notes:** A "Must Visit" if you are in Bageshwar.

### 4. Trekking Base Camps (Critical Info)
* **Pindari Glacier:** Starts from Song/Loharkhet (36 km drive from Bageshwar).
* **Sunderdhunga Glacier:** Starts from Song/Loharkhet.
* **Kafni Glacier:** Starts from Song/Loharkhet.
* **Note:** Bageshwar is the last major town to buy trekking supplies (ration, fuel, medicines) before heading to Song.

## üìÖ Fairs, Festivals & Culture
* **Uttarayani Fair (The Big One):** Held in **January (Makar Sankranti)**.
    * **Significance:** Historically significant; this is where the "Coolie Begar" (forced labor) practice was ended in 1921.
    * **Activity:** Biggest fair in Kumaon. Traders come from Tibet/Nepal borders. Massive crowds.
* **Shivratri:** Held in **Feb/March**. Huge gathering at Bagnath Temple.

## üöó Logistics, Traffic & Parking (Critical)
* **Traffic Pattern:** Bageshwar is a valley town. It gets very hot in summer compared to Almora/Kausani.
* **Parking Situation:** Congested in the main market.
* **Official Parking Spots:**
    1.  **Saryu Bagad Parking:** On the river bank (dry season only).
    2.  **Degree College Ground:** During fairs.
* **Fuel:** Reliable petrol pumps available in the town (Indian Oil / HP). **Fill up here** if heading towards Pithoragarh or Munsiyari (long remote stretches ahead).
